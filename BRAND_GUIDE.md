# KrishakMart Brand Guide
## "Mitti Se Digital Tak" (From Soil to Digital)

---

## 🎨 Brand Colors

### Primary Color - Forest Green
Symbolizes growth, trust, and agriculture
- **Main**: `#2f7c4f` - Used for primary buttons, navigation, headers
- **Dark**: `#236240` - Hover states, darker accents
- **Light**: `#56b886` - Highlights, success messages
- **Lightest**: `#f0f9f4` - Backgrounds, subtle sections

### Secondary Color - Soil Brown
Represents "Mitti" (Soil) connection, earthiness
- **Main**: `#b87a47` - Used for accents, category icons, secondary elements
- **Dark**: `#855132` - Footer borders, darker accents
- **Light**: `#cd9968` - Hover states, lighter accents
- **Lightest**: `#f5ede3` - Backgrounds, subtle highlights

### Supporting Colors
- **Accent Green**: `#bae5cd` - Borders, cards
- **White**: `#ffffff` - Backgrounds, text on dark
- **Gray Scale**: For text and neutral elements

---

## 📝 Typography

### Font Family
**Poppins** - Clean, modern, highly readable sans-serif font

### Font Weights
- **Light**: 300 - Subtle text
- **Regular**: 400 - Body text
- **Medium**: 500 - Labels, navigation
- **Semibold**: 600 - Subheadings, emphasis
- **Bold**: 700 - Headings, important text
- **Extra Bold**: 800 - Hero text, major headings

### Usage
- Headings: Bold/Semibold Poppins
- Body text: Regular Poppins
- Buttons: Semibold Poppins
- Tagline: Italic Poppins

---

## 🏷️ Brand Elements

### Logo
- Circular design with leaf, shopping cart, and soil elements
- Forest green border
- Soil brown hands and field
- Always maintain aspect ratio

### Tagline
**"Mitti Se Digital Tak"**
- Always in italic
- Color: Soil brown (#b87a47) or light beige (#f5ede3) on dark backgrounds
- Positioned below or near the KrishakMart name

### Brand Name
**KrishakMart**
- "Krishak" in forest green
- "Mart" in soil brown (when possible)
- Always capitalize K and M

---

## 🎯 UI/UX Guidelines

### Buttons

#### Primary Buttons
- Background: Forest Green (#2f7c4f)
- Text: White
- Hover: Dark Forest Green (#236240)
- Font: Semibold
- Use for: Add to Cart, Login, Submit, Primary CTAs

#### Secondary Buttons
- Border: Forest Green (#2f7c4f)
- Text: Forest Green (#2f7c4f)
- Background: Transparent/White
- Hover: Light green background (#f0f9f4)
- Use for: View All, Cancel, Secondary actions

#### Accent Buttons
- Background: Soil Brown (#b87a47)
- Text: White
- Hover: Dark Soil Brown (#855132)
- Use for: Special offers, seasonal promotions

### Cards & Containers
- Background: White
- Border: Light green (#bae5cd) or light brown (#e9d7c3)
- Border radius: 0.75rem (12px)
- Shadow: Subtle, soft shadows
- Hover: Slight scale or shadow increase

### Navigation
- Background: White
- Border bottom: Light green (#bae5cd)
- Active/Hover links: Forest green (#2f7c4f)
- Sticky positioning for better UX

### Footer
- Background: Gradient from forest green to dark green
- Border top: Soil brown accent
- Text: White/Light green
- Icons: Light beige (#f5ede3)

---

## 🖼️ Imagery Guidelines

### Photography Style
- High-quality photos of real farmers
- Vibrant, natural colors
- Authentic agricultural settings
- Emotional resonance with target audience
- Show diversity in farming practices

### Image Treatment
- Slight overlay on hero images (30% opacity)
- Maintain aspect ratios
- Use rounded corners (12px) for product images
- Ensure good contrast for text overlays

---

## 📐 Layout Principles

### Spacing
- Consistent padding: 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px
- Section spacing: 64px - 96px
- Card spacing: 24px gaps

### Grid System
- Max width: 1280px (7xl container)
- Responsive breakpoints:
  - Mobile: < 640px
  - Tablet: 640px - 1024px
  - Desktop: > 1024px

### Sections
- Hero: Full-width, gradient background
- Content: Contained, white background
- Features: Light green background (#f0f9f4)
- CTAs: Soil brown gradient

## 🚀 Implementation

All brand colors and styles are implemented in:
- `frontend/src/styles/theme.css` - CSS variables
- `frontend/src/styles/fonts.css` - Typography
- Component files - Applied throughout the UI

### CSS Variables
```css
--forest-green: #2f7c4f;
--soil-brown: #b87a47;
--primary: #2f7c4f;
--secondary: #b87a47;
```
