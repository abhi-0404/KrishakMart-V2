## 🎯 Brand Message

**"Mitti Se Digital Tak"** (From Soil to Digital)

This tagline perfectly captures your mission:
- **Mitti (Soil)** - Traditional farming roots
- **Digital** - Modern e-commerce platform
- **Tak (To)** - The bridge you provide

The colors reinforce this:
- **Forest Green** - Growth, trust, agriculture
- **Soil Brown** - Earth, authenticity, connection

## 💡 Design Philosophy

### Colors Tell a Story
- **Green** represents the crops, growth, and prosperity
- **Brown** represents the soil, foundation, and authenticity
- Together they symbolize the complete farming ecosystem

### Typography Matters
- **Poppins** is modern yet approachable
- **Semibold** weights add confidence
- **Italic tagline** adds personality

### Consistency Builds Trust
- Same colors across all pages
- Predictable button styles
- Familiar navigation patterns
